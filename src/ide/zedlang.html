Instruction Sequence

Assignment, Output, WHEN, REPEAT, FOR, IF, SWITCH

WHEN loop


= I1 0:
WHEN < I1 9
  DO
    = I1 1+:
    OUT[I1]:
  ENDDO
ENDWHEN

REPEAT loop


  = I1 0:
  REPEAT
    DO
      = I1 1+:
      OUT[I1]:
    ENDDO
    UNTIL > I1 9
  ENDWHEN

FOR loop


FOR I1 FROM 1 TO 10 BY 1
  DO
    OUT[I1]:
  ENDDO
ENDFOR
